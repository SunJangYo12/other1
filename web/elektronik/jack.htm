Mono plug (TS)

SIG = Signal (tip)
GND = Ground (sleeve)

Stereo plug (TRS)

L = Left channel (tip)
R = Right channel (ring)
GND = Ground (sleeve)

Balanced mono (TRS)

+ = Positive phase (tip)
- = Negative phase (ring)
GND = Ground (sleeve)

Stereo plug 4-pole

L = Left channel (tip)
R = Right channel (ring)
AUX = Auxiliary signal (ring)
GND = Ground (sleeve)

Used by Nokia and in some old phones. Other devices use "Universal 4-pole cell phone headset" (AUX and GND are inverted)

Universal 4-pole cell phone headset

L = Left channel (tip)
R = Right channel (ring)
GND = Ground (ring)
MIC = Microphone & Control (sleeve)

Used in most cell phones and tablets (HTC, Samsung, Blackberry, Sony, Motorola, iPhone, iPod)
