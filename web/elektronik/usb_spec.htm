USB 3.1 Type-C connector supports higher power devices (5V @ 1.5A, 3A).
USB Power Delivery is configurable up to 20 V and 5 A.

Decoupling capacitance between VBUS and ground on devices:
- Maximum is 10uF to limit inrush current.
- Minimum is 1uF to limit flyback voltage on disconnection.
