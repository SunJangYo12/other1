Notes:
BeagleBone Rev. 6 pinout.
All signals on the expansion are 3.3V.
PWR_BUT is a 5V level as pulled up internally by the TPS65217B.

BeagleBone Black Rev. 5.2 pinout.
BeagleBone Black does not include Expansion Header P6.
The LCD pins (P8: 27-46) are used on the BeagleBone Black to drive the HDMI framer.
The eMMC pins (P8: 11-21) can be used to connect eMMC device.
