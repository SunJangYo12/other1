(*) Powered-B USB connector allows USB accessories to draw power from peripheral devices. It is deprecated in USB 3.1 and replaced by Power Delivery (PD) specs.
Pins 10-11 are used in PD receptacle to detect a PD plug.
Standard connectors don't have the pins 10-11.

The pin-out is independent of plug orientation.
Connector includes both USB 2.0 (D+ and D−) and USB 3.1 (TX and RX pairs) data buses, USB power (VBUS) and ground (GND), Configuration Channel signals (CC1 and CC2), and two Sideband Use (SBU) signal pins.
Not all signals are required in all platforms or devices.
